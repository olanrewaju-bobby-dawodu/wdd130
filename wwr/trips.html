Rafting Trips

Kampar River, Gopeng, Perak

A 7 kilometer journey down the Kampar River near Gopeng which is just a short drive from Ipoh is suitable for beginners and intermediate rafters. Paddle through lush rainforests, face thrilling rapids while enjoying the serene moments between adrenaline rushes. Ideal for team-building and family groups, the Kampar River offers 22 exciting rapids to choose from. Professional guidews certified in swift water rescues and whitewater safety ensure a safe and exciting experience. Packages include essential equipment, safety briefings, lunch, post-rafting relaxation with bonfire and BBQ. Perfect for a full day adventure.

Kiulu River, Sabah

The Kiulu River in Sabah offers a unique rafting experience. Its gentler yet enjoyable river flow is a great option for families and individuals looking for a more laid-back adventure. Ideal for those new to whitewater rafting with Grade 1 to 2 rapids that flow along a scenic 15 kilometer stretch provides ample oppportunities to admire Sabah's breathtaking mountain landscapes, villages and rainforests. The trip includes certified guides, equipment, transport to and from Kota Kinabalu

Selangor River, Kuala Kubu Bharu, Selangor

The Selangor River offers an adventurous yet scenic whitewater rafting experience with its Grade 3 rapids. Suitable for those with basic rafting experience while remaining accesssible to beginners looking for a challenge. This rafting experience involves navigating fast-flowing waters intersped with calm sections. Professional guidwes provide safety briefings and all necessary safety and rafting equipment. With its combination of adventure and scenic beauty, its a perfect half-day getaway for groups and individuals alike to soak in the jungle surroundings.
